# OpenSpec

## Definition
Lightweight, iterative specification framework for AI-assisted development. Focused on structured specs with execution, less ceremony than BMAD.

## Why It Matters
Offers a middle ground between vibe coding (no specs) and BMAD (full Agile simulation). Good for teams that want spec discipline without multi-agent overhead.

## Key Ideas
- Light iterative specs
- Structured execution
- Less governance than Spec-Kit
- Suitable for teams wanting minimal ceremony
