# AI-Human Division of Labor

## Definition
A knowledge management model where AI handles mechanical organization (tagging, cross-referencing, structuring) while humans focus on curation, judgment, and quality control.

## Why It Matters
Optimizes both sides: AI scales the grunt work, humans provide the quality gate. Prevents [[concepts/Editorial-Drift]] while still leveraging AI's organizational capacity.

## Key Ideas
- AI: tagging, linking, structuring, compiling views
- Human: curation, fact-checking, direction, quality gates
- Reduces cognitive load on the human without surrendering control
- Works best with [[concepts/Knowledge-Source-of-Truth]] architecture
- The human is the editor-in-chief, AI is the staff writer

## Tradeoffs
- Requires clear role boundaries
- Human must stay engaged for quality control
