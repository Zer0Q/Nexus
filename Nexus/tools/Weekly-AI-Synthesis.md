# Weekly AI Synthesis

## Definition
An automated recurring process where an AI model reviews all new content added to a knowledge base during a fixed period (typically one week) and produces a structured synthesis: connections between unrelated notes, emerging patterns, contradictions, and highest-value items.

## Why It Matters
Manual weekly reviews are slow (30-45 minutes) and tend to produce shallow results -- mostly reminding yourself that information exists. AI synthesis is faster, more thorough, and can surface non-obvious connections across the full vault.

## Key Ideas
- Four output sections: Connections, Patterns, Contradictions, Highest-value source
- Connections must be non-obvious (if you would have noticed it yourself, it does not qualify)
- Patterns require 3+ notes showing the same theme
- Contradictions are flagged but not resolved -- the tension is the insight
- Runs automatically (scheduled) so it does not depend on motivation
- Quality improves as the vault grows and has more material to connect

## Tradeoffs
- Requires consistent capture to have meaningful input
- Prompt needs periodic tuning as vault matures
- May produce false connections in early stages when vault is thin
