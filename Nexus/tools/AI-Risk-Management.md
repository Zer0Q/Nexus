# AI Risk Management

ISO/IEC 23894:2023 extends ISO 31000 risk management to the AI lifecycle, addressing emergent risks unique to non-deterministic AI systems.

## AI-Specific Risk Domains
- **Algorithmic Bias and Fairness** -- systematic discrimination in training data
- **Explainability Gap** -- reputational and operational liability of black-box decisions
- **Model Performance Degradation** -- [[concepts/Model-Drift]] as real-world data shifts
- **Adversarial Vulnerabilities** -- data poisoning and model evasion

## Lifecycle Integration
1. **Inception & Design** -- feasibility, limitations, misuse scenarios
2. **Data & Model Development** -- data validation, representativeness, legal compliance
3. **Continuous Monitoring** -- governance workflows tracking behavior under production stress
