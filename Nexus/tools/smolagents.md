# smolagents

## Definition
Hugging Face's lightweight SDK for building simple AI agents. Focused on prototyping, tool usage, and workflow automation rather than full SDLC coverage.

## Why It Matters
Low-barrier entry to building custom AI agents. Good for prototyping agent behaviors and integrating tools without the overhead of full frameworks.

## Key Ideas
- Simple agent construction
- Tool integration focus
- Prototyping and workflow automation
- Not a complete SDLC solution
