# NotebookLM

## Definition
Google's AI-powered research tool that grounds responses in user-provided source documents, enabling fact-based Q&A over custom knowledge bases.

## Why It Matters
Unlike general-purpose LLMs that hallucinate, NotebookLM stays grounded in provided sources — making it reliable for research, study, and document analysis.

## Key Ideas
- Upload documents (PDFs, text, articles, slides)
- AI generates summaries, Q&A, and audio overviews
- Responses cite specific source passages
- Audio overview feature creates podcast-style discussions
- Best used as a research supplement, not replacement for reading

## Tradeoffs
- Limited to uploaded sources (no web search)
- Source quality directly affects output quality
- Not suitable for creative or open-ended tasks
