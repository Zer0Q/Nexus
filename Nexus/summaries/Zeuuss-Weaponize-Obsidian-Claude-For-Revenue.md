# Weaponize Obsidian + Claude for Revenue

## Summary
zeuuss_01 argues that most Obsidian vaults earn $0 because they optimize capture while leaving output unstaffed. The solution is a four-zone vault built backward from revenue-producing output, powered by five automated workflows and a five-tool AI stack. The real moat is not better tools or prompts but shipping discipline -- consistently producing publishable, billable work from accumulated context over six months.

## Key Insights
- The capture/use gap is structural: most vaults optimize the inbox but leave the exit unstaffed
- A vault that earns money is optimized backward from the output, not forward from capture
- Four-zone asymmetry: most of the day lives in projects and output, not capture
- The Telegram bot is the highest-ROI capture surface (captures ideas otherwise lost forever)
- Five workflows run on the vault; only the Output Generator produces revenue directly, the other four feed it
- The $30K breakdown: newsletter $5K, consulting $10K, productized service $8K, affiliate $7K
- You don't need all four revenue lines -- any two reliably hit $13-25K/month
- The five-tool stack assigns each tool to one layer; using one tool for all five is the common mistake
- The 6-month revenue curve is the moat: anyone can install Obsidian, few persist past 90 days
- Shipping discipline cannot be handed to you -- it is the one non-replicable element
