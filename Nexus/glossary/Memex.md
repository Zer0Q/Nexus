# Memex

Vannevar Bush's 1945 vision of a personal knowledge store with associative trails between documents. Private, actively curated, with connections between documents as valuable as the documents themselves.

- Closer to LLM wiki pattern than to what the web became
- Bush couldn't solve who does the maintenance -- the LLM handles that now
- "Associative trail" = modern wikilinks
