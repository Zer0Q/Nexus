# KGrEaT (Knowledge Graph Evaluation on Downstream Tasks)

Extrinsic validation framework that judges knowledge graph quality based on its practical performance when integrated into real-world AI tasks, including semantic concept classification, clustering accuracy, and recommendation engine precision.

- Task-based evaluation rather than structural or triple-level analysis
- Measures real utility: how well the graph performs in production applications
- Complements LP-Measure (intrinsic structure) and KGTtm (intrinsic truthfulness)
