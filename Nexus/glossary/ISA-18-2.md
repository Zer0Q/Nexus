# ISA-18.2

ANSI/ISA-18.2-2016: Management of Alarm Systems for the Process Industries.

- Authoritative alarm management standard (nearly identical to IEC 62682)
- Lifecycle approach: philosophy, identification, rationalization, design, implementation, operation, maintenance, monitoring, MOC, audit
- Supported by 8 technical reports (TR18.2.1 through TR18.2.8)
- Recognized by OSHA, EPA, FDA, PHMSA as RAGAGEP
