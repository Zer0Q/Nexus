# Alarm Rationalization

Systematic review and justification of potential alarms against defined criteria.

- Removes notifications that are not truly alarms
- Documents consequence, response time, operator action for each alarm
- Output: Master Alarm Database (MAD) with configuration requirements
- Typical result: 50% of alarms removed/added, 80% modified
- Covered by ISA-TR18.2.2
