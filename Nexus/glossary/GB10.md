# GB10

The Grace Blackwell superchip at the heart of the NVIDIA DGX Spark. Combines a 20-core ARM CPU (Grace) with Blackwell GPU architecture in a single chip, delivering 1 PFLOP of FP4 AI throughput with 128GB of unified LPDDR5x memory shared between CPU and GPU.

- CPU: 20-core ARM (Grace)
- GPU: Blackwell architecture (~RTX 5070-class cores)
- Unified memory: 128GB LPDDR5x
- AI throughput: 1 PFLOP FP4
