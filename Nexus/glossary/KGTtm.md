# KGTtm (Knowledge Graph Triple Trustworthiness Measurement)

Model for evaluating the intrinsic truthfulness and certainty level of each semantic triple extracted from source texts. Ensures elimination of spurious logical connections generated by deficient inference processes.

- Scores individual triples rather than the graph as a whole
- Focuses on factual accuracy of each relationship
- Complements LP-Measure (structural consistency) with factual reliability
