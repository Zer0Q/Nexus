# EU AI Act Article 4

## Definition
EU AI Act Article 4 is the provision requiring providers and deployers of AI systems to ensure a sufficient level of AI literacy among staff and other people dealing with AI systems on their behalf.

## Why It Matters
Article 4 turns AI literacy from a best practice into a compliance obligation. Organizations must connect training to actual AI use, role, context, and risk, then keep evidence that they did so.

## Key Ideas
- Applies broadly to providers and deployers, not only high-risk AI providers.
- Literacy measures should reflect technical knowledge, experience, education, context, and AI system risk.
- Documentation and record-keeping are part of the control.
- Service-provider relationships can create shared literacy obligations.
