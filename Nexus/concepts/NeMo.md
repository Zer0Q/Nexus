# NeMo

NVIDIA's framework for building, training, and deploying generative AI models. Pre-installed on DGX OS alongside CUDA, NIM, and the full NVIDIA AI stack. Enables fine-tuning and custom model development on the DGX Spark.

- Part of the DGX OS preloaded stack
- Supports model training and fine-tuning
- Used for custom model development on local hardware
- Complements inference tools like Ollama and vLLM
