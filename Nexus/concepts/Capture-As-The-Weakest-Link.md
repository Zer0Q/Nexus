# Capture As The Weakest Link

## Definition
The principle that a knowledge management system's quality is entirely dependent on the consistency of its capture layer. If capture breaks down, the entire system degrades within two weeks regardless of how sophisticated the processing or synthesis layers are.

## Why It Matters
People over-invest in processing tools (AI synthesis, tagging, linking) while under-investing in capture. A perfect system with zero input is worthless. Capture must be frictionless enough to survive bad weeks.

## Key Ideas
- Capture is the single point of failure in any knowledge system
- System degrades within two weeks of inconsistent capture
- Capture infrastructure must remove every possible excuse for not saving something
- Cross-device, one-tap capture is the gold standard (e.g., Telegram bot)
- Processing can be delayed; capture cannot

## Tradeoffs
- Over-optimising capture can lead to saving everything indiscriminately
- Need to balance capture speed with some quality filter
