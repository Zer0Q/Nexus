## Definition

Token capital is the collection of AI workflows, decision patterns, learned skills, and institutional knowledge a company builds on top of foundation models. It compounds with human capital: every improved workflow generates better signal, which produces sharper AI behavior, which frees human attention for higher-judgment work.

## Key Insight

"The moat isn't the model — it's the loop." A company should be able to switch out a 'generalist' model without losing the 'company veteran' expertise built into their learning system. Durable skills don't care which LLM calls them.

## Why Durability Matters

If skills die on process restart, the compounding resets to zero. Durable execution is what makes the token capital investment persist across process failures, model swaps, and team changes.
