# Forward Deployed Engineering

## Definition
Engineers embedded at customer sites or closely integrated with customer workflows to bridge the gap between product capabilities and real-world usage, especially critical for AI/ML products.

## Why It Matters
As intelligence becomes commoditized, competitive advantage shifts from model quality to deployment effectiveness. FDEs ensure AI solutions actually work in customer environments.

## Key Ideas
- FDEs sit between product engineering and customer success
- They adapt AI solutions to specific customer data and workflows
- Deep technical skills + customer empathy required
- Hot role at Anthropic, OpenAI, Google for enterprise AI
- Unlike traditional SEs, FDEs write production code at customer sites

## Tradeoffs
- Context switching between customers reduces depth
- Hard to scale — each customer needs tailored attention
- Career path less clear than pure engineering or sales
