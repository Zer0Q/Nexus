# AI Monetization Paths

## Definition
Three proven paths to monetize AI expertise in 2026: Distribution (content + audience), Consulting (SMB AI implementation), and Product (AI-native tools and services).

## Why It Matters
AI knowledge alone doesn't generate revenue. Structured monetization paths turn technical expertise into sustainable income streams.

## Key Ideas
- Distribution Play: build audience through content, monetize via sponsors/courses
- Consulting Play: help SMBs implement AI workflows (highest near-term revenue)
- Product Play: build AI-native tools (highest long-term upside)
- Career progression: Career Architect → Income Architect → Venture Architect
- Paid communities and cohorts as recurring revenue

## Tradeoffs
- Distribution takes time to build
- Consulting doesn't scale linearly
- Product requires technical + business skills
