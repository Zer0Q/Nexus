# SOUL.md

Static identity file for AI agents defining personality, tone, communication style, and hard limits. Loaded as slot #1 in the system prompt, before memory or skills. Hand-authored and consistent across sessions. Beyond personality, it is the instruction layer governing how the agent interprets everything it reads in your vault.

## Three Critical Sections
- **Current Obsessions**: specific active research questions, not general interests
- **Challenge Instructions**: how you want the agent to push back (surface contradictions, flag assumptions)
- **Negative Constraints**: hard rules about what the agent must never produce (no flattery, no padding, no generic commentary)

A vague SOUL.md produces vague outputs. A specific SOUL.md makes the agent read your vault through the lens of who you are and what you are trying to do.
