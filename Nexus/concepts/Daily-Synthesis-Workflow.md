# Daily Synthesis Workflow

## Definition
An automated morning briefing where AI reads recent captures (last 7 days) and produces a synthesis with four sections: connections (non-obvious links between notes), patterns (themes across 3+ notes), contradictions (conflicting positions), and open questions (recurring unresolved questions).

## Why It Matters
Transforms the vault from passive storage to active thinking partner. The connections section alone generates more original ideas than manual brainstorming. Quality compounds over weeks as the AI accumulates context about your thinking patterns.

## Key Ideas
- Runs automatically every morning (e.g., 6am via N8N)
- Four sections: connections, patterns, contradictions, open questions
- Each item must trace back to a specific note -- no generic insight
- "Never summarize. Always synthesize."
- Weekly sessions look across 30 days for deeper patterns
- At 8 weeks: AI knows which arguments you return to, which questions unresolved

## Tradeoffs
- Token cost scales with capture volume
- Quality depends on CLAUDE.md/VELLUM.md specificity
- Risk of repetitive connections as system matures
