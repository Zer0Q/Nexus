# Alarm Flood

A condition where the alarm system generates alarms faster than the operator can respond, overwhelming situational awareness.

- Caused by cascading process upsets or poorly configured alarm thresholds
- Critical alarms get lost in the noise
- Dynamic alarming and alarm shelving help manage flood conditions
- ISA-18.2 defines performance metrics to detect and prevent floods
