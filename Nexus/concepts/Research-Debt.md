# Research Debt

## Definition
Undigested ideas that accumulate in a field when researchers produce results without clear explanations. A clear explanation is a genuine contribution, not a service job. Public writing serves as the strongest unfakeable credential because it is a sample of how you think.

## Why It Matters
Fields choke on undigested ideas when everyone produces papers but nobody synthesizes the connections. A body of public writing doubles as the strongest credential you can hold — it is an unfakeable sample of how you think. Many people working in interpretability today found the field through readable posts, not conference papers.

## Key Ideas
- Olah and Carter's research debt essay: fields choke on undigested ideas
- Clear explanations are genuine contributions, not service jobs
- Public writing is the strongest unfakeable credential — a sample of how you think
- Writing finds gaps your head papers over: untested assumptions, steps that don't follow, quiet contradictions
- Feynman's rule: the first person you must avoid fooling is yourself, because you are the easiest target
- Darwin's procedural defense: write down any fact that cuts against your theory on the spot — your memory deletes inconvenient evidence faster than convenient kind

## Tradeoffs
- Writing takes time away from running experiments
- Public writing exposes half-formed ideas to criticism before they are ready
- The pressure to publish papers can incentivize results over understanding
