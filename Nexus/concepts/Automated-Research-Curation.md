# Automated Research Curation

## Definition
The use of AI agents to automatically discover, summarize, and integrate research materials into a knowledge base. Includes monitoring feeds, extracting key insights, and creating structured notes from raw research content.

## Why It Matters
Scales the research intake pipeline beyond what's manually feasible, ensuring that valuable information from papers, articles, and discussions is captured and organized before it's forgotten.

## Key Ideas
- AI agents can monitor RSS feeds, arXiv, Twitter for relevant content
- Automated extraction creates consistent source notes and concept entries
- Tools like qmd and LLM-Wiki pattern enable programmatic knowledge ingestion
