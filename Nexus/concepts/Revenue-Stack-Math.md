# Revenue Stack Math

## Definition
A breakdown of how a knowledge vault produces $30K/month through four revenue streams: newsletter ($5K), consulting ($10K), productized service ($8K), and affiliate content ($7K). None require exceptional skill -- they require consistent shipping from a compounding vault.

## Why It Matters
Most people treat a knowledge vault as a personal tool. This framework reframes it as a revenue-generating asset with predictable income streams. You do not need all four lines -- hitting any two reliably reaches $13-25K/month.

## Key Ideas
- **Newsletter ($5K/month)**: Consistent output from vault insights, monetized via subscriptions/sponsorships
- **Consulting ($10K/month)**: Vault-backed expertise delivered as client strategy sessions
- **Productized service ($8K/month)**: Repeatable deliverables generated from vault workflows
- **Affiliate content ($7K/month)**: Blog posts, reviews, recommendations sourced from accumulated research
- Each line requires shipping from a vault that compounds over time
- You do not need all four -- any two reliably hit $13-25K/month
- The vault produces the content; discipline produces the revenue

## Tradeoffs
- Revenue scales with the age and depth of the vault (cold start problem)
- Each revenue stream requires different output types and audiences
- Affiliate income depends on platform algorithms and link performance
