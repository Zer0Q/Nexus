# Design System First

## Definition
Building a complete design system -- colors, typography, components, spacing, tokens -- before designing any screens. The design system determines output quality; garbage in, garbage out. AI design tools extract design language from Figma, GitHub, live URLs, brand descriptions, or design inspiration.

## Why It Matters
Without a design system, every AI-generated screen drifts from the last one -- colors shift, typography changes, components become inconsistent. By the fifth feature, the app looks like five different products stitched together.

## Key Ideas
- Design system is the step everyone skips but the most important
- Import from Figma, GitHub, live URL, or describe brand to AI builder
- Full component library with live preview -- every badge, button, card rendered
- Gallery view: see all component variants side by side for informed decisions
- Design system quality determines output quality across every screen
- Takes ~20 minutes with AI tools vs two days with a designer

## Tradeoffs
- Upfront investment in system vs speed of first screen
- System flexibility vs consistency -- rigid systems prevent creative deviations
- AI-extracted systems vs hand-crafted -- AI systems may miss nuanced brand decisions
