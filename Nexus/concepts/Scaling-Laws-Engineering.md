# Scaling Laws Engineering

## Definition
Patrones y lecciones al escalar equipos de ingeniería: cuándo los procesos rompen, cómo adaptarlos, y qué métricas monitorizar para detectar problemas de escala antes de que sean críticos.

## Why It Matters
Cada milestone de escala (10→50→100→200 personas) requiere adaptaciones diferentes en processes, structures, y culture. Los founders técnicos que prefieren computers sobre people necesitan frameworks para navegar esto.

## Key Ideas
- "Computers are deterministic. People are emotional." — founders técnicos enfrentan this transition
- "if you find real PMF, team building becomes unavoidable"
- "You have to build more. You have to move faster. You have to do more things in parallel."
- Company deja de ser product que small group brute-forcea → organization que needs improvement sin breaking
- Hiring metrics como diagnostic: pass rates, interviewer variance, stage conversion, close rates
- Watch for patterns: repeated short stints, scale-seen vs scale-doners, pedigree bias
- "The flags you see during interview process almost always show up 10x at work"

## Tradeoffs
- Process rigor vs flexibility a different scales
- Centralized vs decentralized decision-making at scale
- Culture preservation vs need for fresh perspectives
- Hiring speed vs quality at different growth stages
