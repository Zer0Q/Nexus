# Second Brain Architect

## Definition
A professional role at the intersection of knowledge management and AI engineering. Second Brain Architects design AI-powered knowledge management systems that make human thinking and AI reasoning work together — organizing knowledge, connecting it with AI, and creating self-maintaining workflows.

## Why It Matters
Every company runs on knowledge that is scattered across abandoned docs, old chat threads, and tribal knowledge. The demand for people who can build organized, AI-connected knowledge systems is exploding while supply is nearly nonexistent — it requires combining PKM philosophy, Obsidian expertise, AI integration, and context engineering.

## Key Ideas
- Six-month learning path: foundations → AI integration → automation → context engineering → building for others → professionalization
- Revenue range: $500 basic setup to $2,000-$5,000 comprehensive system build, plus monthly retainers
- Three differentiators: systems over tools, AI-first design, relentless iteration
- Titles emerging: Knowledge Engineer, AI Workflow Designer, PKM Consultant, AI Operations Specialist
- Market signals: Obsidian 1.5M users (22% YoY), Claude Code $2.5B ARR
- The skill requires all four competencies: PKM philosophy, tool expertise, AI integration, context engineering

## Tradeoffs
- Building for yourself vs building for others requires different design priorities
- Reusable starter kits vs custom solutions for each client
