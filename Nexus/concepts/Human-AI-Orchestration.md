# Human-AI Orchestration

## Definition
The division of labor between human judgment and AI execution within knowledge workflows. Humans handle creative synthesis, editorial decisions, and quality control, while AI handles capture, organization, summarization, and connection discovery.

## Why It Matters
Maximizes the strengths of both: human intuition and contextual understanding combined with AI's ability to process, organize, and connect large volumes of information.

## Key Ideas
- AI handles the mechanical: capture, tagging, linking, summarizing
- Humans handle the creative: synthesis, editorial judgment, novel connections
- The orchestration pattern scales personal knowledge systems beyond manual capacity
