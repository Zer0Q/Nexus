# Active-Decision-Feeder

## Definition
A workflow triggered when making a decision that scans the entire Zone 2 vault for relevant notes -- not just those explicitly tagged with the decision, but any note containing information, perspectives, or evidence worth considering.

## Why It Matters
The answer to your current question may already exist in past captures across different contexts, sources, and weeks. This workflow surfaces that latent knowledge without requiring you to know it exists or remember to search for it.

## Key Steps
1. State the decision being made
2. Scan all of Zone 2 for relevant notes (beyond explicit tags)
3. For each relevant note found: state title, what it contains that is relevant, whether it supports/challenges/adds nuance
4. Synthesize all relevant notes into a decision brief
5. Only use information already in the vault; do not add external information

## Tradeoffs
- Requires a sufficiently populated Zone 2; value compounds over time
- May surface tangentially related notes that dilute focus
- Most valuable after 90 days of accumulated notes
