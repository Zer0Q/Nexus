## Definition

Context curation is the practice of assembling and maintaining the right context for AI agents before starting any task. Output quality is directly proportional to context quality.

## Effective Context Components

- **The user:** Real details, not personas. Who they are, what they care about, what makes them give up
- **The problem in their words:** Direct quotes from calls, tickets, sales notes
- **What good looks like:** Examples your team considers well-designed
- **What you've tried and why it failed:** Institutional knowledge that usually lives in people's heads
- **Constraints that shape the solution:** Only the ones that will actually change what gets built
- **How you'll know it worked:** Concrete, measurable outcomes

## Key Insight

"When I ask an agent to prototype something now, it's not starting from zero. It knows who we're building for, what they actually said, what good looks like, and what's already failed. The output fits because the input was specific."
