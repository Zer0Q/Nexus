# Risk-by-Design

Proactive methodology that integrates risk management into every phase of the AI lifecycle, shifting from reactive post-deployment fixes to upfront risk consideration.

## Core Principles
- Risk assessment starts at inception, not after deployment
- Acceptable risk thresholds defined before development
- Clear ownership boundaries and escalation workflows
- Continuous monitoring replaces point-in-time assessments

## Application
- Embed [[AI-Risk-Management]] into the Secure Development Life Cycle (SDLC)
- Aligns with [[Spec-Driven-Development]] -- risk constraints defined in specs
- Enables faster innovation by defining safe boundaries upfront
