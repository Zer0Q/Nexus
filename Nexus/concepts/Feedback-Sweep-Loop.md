# Feedback Sweep Loop

## Definition
A Feedback Sweep Loop collects user complaints, corrections, scores, and preferences across recurring workflows, groups them by failure pattern, and turns them into ranked improvement tasks.

## Why It Matters
Self-improving agent workflows need structured feedback, not scattered vibes. A feedback sweep supplies the backlog that a champion loop or prompt-improvement process can safely work through.

## Key Ideas
- Listen for repeated feedback across outputs and workflows.
- Cluster complaints by workflow and failure mode.
- Rank fixes by frequency, severity, or business impact.
- Feed one focused issue at a time into a controlled improvement loop.
