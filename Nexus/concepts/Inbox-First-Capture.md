# Inbox-First Capture

A capture strategy where all incoming information lands in a single unorganised inbox before being categorised later. Separates fast capture from slow organisation.

- Capture speed must be under 10 seconds
- Inbox is temporary, not a destination
- Processing happens during scheduled review
- Prevents bookmark graveyards
