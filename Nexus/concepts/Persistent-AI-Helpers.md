# Persistent AI Helpers

## Definition
AI agents that maintain persistent context across sessions, acting as long-term assistants within a knowledge system. Unlike stateless chat interactions, these helpers remember previous conversations, vault structure, and user preferences.

## Why It Matters
Enables AI to act as a true knowledge partner rather than a one-off tool — understanding your vault's structure, your writing style, and your research interests over time.

## Key Ideas
- Persistent context allows AI to improve suggestions over time
- Skills/plugins give AI repeatable workflows tailored to specific vaults
- Bridge between ephemeral AI chat and durable knowledge systems
