# FP4

4-bit floating point precision format used for AI compute throughput measurement. The DGX Spark's 1 PFLOP rating is in FP4 ops/second — a quadrillion operations. FP4 is lower precision than FP16 or BF16 but enables higher throughput for inference workloads where extreme precision isn't required.

- 1 PFLOP = 1 quadrillion FP4 ops/second
- Lower precision than FP16/BF16
- Higher throughput for inference at the cost of precision
- Common metric for comparing AI accelerator performance
