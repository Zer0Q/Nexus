# SOUL-MD Challenge Instructions

## Definition
Explicit instructions in SOUL.md telling the agent how to challenge your thinking -- specifically to surface contradictions between your current beliefs and your saved notes, flag assumptions, and push back on confident but unverified claims.

## Why It Matters
AI assistants are trained to be agreeable. Without explicit challenge instructions, the agent will confirm your biases rather than test them. The most valuable thing a vault-connected agent does is find gaps between what you think you know and what your own notes say.

## Key Ideas
- Explicitly instruct the agent to look for contradictions
- Example: "Surface contradictions between my current beliefs and my saved notes"
- The agent quotes both the current belief and the conflicting note
- This is the highest-value function of a vault-aware agent
- Only works if SOUL.md explicitly asks for it

## Tradeoffs
- Can feel uncomfortable -- the agent is designed to disagree with you
- May surface false contradictions if notes are ambiguous
- Requires honest notes (garbage in produces garbage challenges)
