# HMI (Human-Machine Interface)

Graphical interface through which operators monitor and control industrial processes.

- Traditional HMIs: colorful, cluttered, PID-style graphics
- High-Performance HMI: grayscale base, color for abnormal, information over data
- Design standard: ISA-101 (ANSI/ISA-101.01-2015)
- Poorly designed HMIs have contributed to industrial accidents
