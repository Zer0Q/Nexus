# Narrative Signal Detection

## Definition
Automated detection of emerging patterns across weeks of research captures that point toward a narrative worth positioning on before it surfaces on social media or public discourse.

## Why It Matters
In fast-moving domains (crypto, tech, markets), the value of an insight decays as more people notice it. Narrative signal detection catches patterns forming across your private research notes before they become consensus -- giving you a timing advantage.

## Key Ideas
- Specifically designed to catch what Claude-only briefs miss: patterns across weeks of captures
- The morning brief includes a dedicated "narrative signals" section
- Looks for themes building across multiple captures that haven't been named yet
- Outputs are actionable: "this pattern suggests positioning on X before CT notices"
- Quality improves with vault depth and SOUL.md specificity

## Tradeoffs
- Requires consistent capture discipline (garbage in, garbage out)
- Signal detection can produce false positives in noisy domains
- The value proposition is strongest in fast-moving, information-dense fields
