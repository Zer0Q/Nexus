# Four-Zone Monetization Vault

## Definition
A vault structure collapsed into four zones optimized backward from revenue-producing output: 00-CAPTURE (raw intake), 01-CONTEXT (processed knowledge), 02-PROJECTS (active work), 03-OUTPUT (publishable/billable deliverables). The asymmetry is deliberate: most of the day lives in 02 and 03.

## Why It Matters
Most vaults optimize the inbox and leave the exit unstaffed. This structure forces the vault to be output-first: if 03-OUTPUT/ has zero notes this week, the vault is a hobby not a business. The four zones collapse complexity while making the revenue path explicit.

## Key Ideas
- **00-CAPTURE**: Raw intake, no categorization at capture time. Claude categorizes later.
- **01-CONTEXT**: Processed, classified, frontmatter-tagged notes from the nightly processor.
- **02-PROJECTS**: Active workspaces for each revenue-generating initiative.
- **03-OUTPUT**: Shippable deliverables -- newsletters, consulting docs, product drafts, affiliate content.
- You should know how many notes were generated in 03-OUTPUT/ this week without thinking about it.
- 07-SYSTEM/ holds prompts and configuration outside the four zones.

## Tradeoffs
- Fewer zones mean less organizational nuance for complex multi-domain vaults
- Requires discipline to keep captures flowing through the pipeline instead of staying in 00
- The output zone can accumulate drafts that never ship if shipping discipline is weak
