# P&ID (Piping and Instrumentation Diagram)

Technical drawing showing process equipment, piping, and instrumentation connections.

- Traditional HMIs were simple depictions of P&IDs
- High-Performance HMI uses P&ID-based layouts but focuses on operator realm of control and KPIs
- Not the same as HMI display: P&ID shows physical layout, HMI shows operational status
