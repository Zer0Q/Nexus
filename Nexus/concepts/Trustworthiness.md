# Trustworthiness

ISO/IEC 22989:2022 decomposes trustworthiness into verifiable characteristics:

- **Robustness** -- maintaining performance under unexpected or adversarial conditions
- **Explainability** -- expressing factors influencing outputs in human-understandable ways
- **Transparency** -- ensuring information about design, data, and limitations is available
