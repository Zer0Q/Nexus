# Three Capture Surfaces

## Definition
Three capture channels designed to cover every possible context where an idea arises: QuickAdd at the desktop (keyboard shortcut to daily note), mobile widget (tap-to-capture on phone), and Telegram bot (voice-message capture when phone is not in hand).

## Why It Matters
Ideas lost at capture are lost forever. The three surfaces ensure zero friction regardless of physical context: at desk, phone in hand, or phone not in hand. The Telegram bot is the highest-ROI surface because it captures ideas during walks, showers, and rides -- contexts disproportionately producing high-value insights.

## Key Ideas
- **Surface 1 -- QuickAdd**: One keyboard shortcut, floating input box, lands in today's daily note. No categorization at capture.
- **Surface 2 -- Mobile widget**: Home screen tap, text or voice-to-text, syncs to vault in 30 seconds.
- **Surface 3 -- Telegram bot**: Voice message to a bot, forwarded to the Captures section of today's daily note.
- No categorization at capture time -- Claude does classification later via the nightly processor.
- The Telegram bot captures ideas you would otherwise lose forever.

## Tradeoffs
- Three surfaces require three integrations to maintain
- Telegram bot requires N8N or similar automation to forward messages to the vault
- Voice-to-text accuracy varies by language and accent
