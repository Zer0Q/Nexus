# Context-Aware AI Research

## Definition
Using an AI model that has been given access to your accumulated knowledge base (notes, sources, project files) so its answers are grounded in your specific context rather than only its general training data.

## Why It Matters
A fresh AI session is smart but ignorant of your work. It cannot flag contradictions in your notes, surface forgotten connections, or build on your previous thinking. Context-aware AI turns a general-purpose tool into a research partner that knows your domain.

## Key Ideas
- AI reads your vault alongside its training data
- Can surface connections between notes written weeks apart
- Flags where existing notes conflict with or support new findings
- Quality depends on the volume and structure of your accumulated notes
- Implemented via project knowledge files, RAG systems, or embedded context windows

## Tradeoffs
- Requires well-structured notes for the AI to actually use them
- Large context windows cost more tokens/compute
- AI may over-weight your notes vs. its own knowledge if not prompted carefully
