# AI Chief of Staff

## Definition
An AI system that reads everything you've read, remembers everything you've forgotten, and briefs you every morning on what matters. Acts as a personal intelligence layer that synthesizes captures, surfaces connections, and tracks unresolved questions.

## Why It Matters
Transforms the relationship with your knowledge base from passive retrieval to active intelligence. The AI doesn't just find what you ask for -- it proactively surfaces what you should know based on your accumulated thinking.

## Key Ideas
- Reads all captures, finds connections across domains
- Morning briefing: connections, patterns, contradictions, questions
- Tracks unresolved questions across weeks/months
- Challenges assumptions before agreeing with them
- "A stale VELLUM.md/CLAUDE.md produces stale output"
- Built with Obsidian + Vellum/Claude + N8N + Readwise + Telegram bot

## Tradeoffs
- Quality depends entirely on context file specificity
- Token costs for daily processing
- Risk of AI becoming an echo chamber if not challenged
