# Local AI Privacy

## Definition
The practice of running AI models on your own hardware so that your notes, ideas, and sensitive data never leave your device. The core question: should your most important thinking live on someone else's server?

## Why It Matters
Every prompt sent to cloud AI is a data transfer. For knowledge workers handling client data, research, financial analysis, or deeply personal thinking, the privacy tradeoff becomes significant. Local AI eliminates this transfer entirely.

## Key Ideas
- Cloud AI = data transfer on every prompt
- Local AI = zero data leaves the machine
- Privacy is not about distrust of AI companies but about data sovereignty
- The threshold for "too sensitive for cloud" varies by profession and personal comfort
- Local AI is now feasible on consumer hardware (modern laptops run 7B-13B models)

## Tradeoffs
- Local models are generally less capable than frontier cloud models
- Requires upfront setup and hardware consideration
- Maintenance burden of updating models manually
