# Cross-Month Connections

## Definition
Linking recent captures to notes created weeks or months earlier, rather than connecting only within the current week. Enabled by agents that read the full vault, not just the last 7 days of inbox content.

## Why It Matters
Most automated synthesis tools look at a narrow window (7 days). The most valuable connections often span longer timeframes -- a note from 6 weeks ago may contradict or reinforce something you captured today. Cross-month connections surface insights that weekly-only systems miss.

## Key Ideas
- Hermes morning brief connects 7-day captures to the entire vault
- The difference between cross-week and cross-month connections is qualitative, not just quantitative
- By week 4 of connected operation, the vault has enough depth for meaningful cross-month links
- Connection quality compounds as the vault grows
- Each cross-month link either validates or challenges existing thinking

## Tradeoffs
- Requires sufficient vault depth (meaningless with fewer than ~30 notes)
- Higher token cost than narrow-window synthesis
- Risk of stale connections if older notes are no longer relevant
