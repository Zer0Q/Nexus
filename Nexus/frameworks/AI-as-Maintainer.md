# AI as Maintainer

## Definition
A paradigm shift where AI transitions from an Oracle (one-off answer provider) to a Maintainer -- an ongoing steward of compounding knowledge artifacts that evolve over time.

## Why It Matters
Changes the AI-human relationship: instead of asking questions and getting answers, the AI continuously curates, organizes, and updates a living knowledge base that compounds in value.

## Key Ideas
- AI handles the "grunt work" of organization and cross-referencing
- Human focuses on curation, judgment, and direction
- Knowledge artifact grows more valuable over time
- Requires [[Knowledge-Source-of-Truth]] to prevent [[Editorial-Drift]]
- Division of labor: AI maintains structure, human maintains quality

## Tradeoffs
- Requires ongoing maintenance, not one-time setup
- Human oversight still needed to prevent drift
