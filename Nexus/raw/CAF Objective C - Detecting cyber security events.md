## Cyber Assessment Framework

The CAF is a collection of cyber security guidance for organisations that play a vital role in the day-to-day life of the UK, with a focus on essential functions.

## CAF Objective C - Detecting cyber security events

![](https://www.ncsc.gov.uk/sites/default/files/images/events.svg)

iStock.com/z\_wei

Capabilities exist to ensure security defences remain effective and to detect cyber security events affecting, or with the potential to affect, essential functions.
